Some cool features of our website and how we coded it

First of all, I would like to talk about a quick feature found all through our website, which is regarding buttons. When you hover over the buttons, the color changes or a shadow gets added to it, due to a pseudo-class called hover.

Next, we used the HTML input tag (specifically type="text", type="email", type="password", etc.) for single-line inputs like your name for the book and appointment section. These text boxes are like areas where you can type in information needed for our code to perform. We also had a required tag to help give an error message if you tried to submit the form with nothing in the text box for the appointment section.

Now for the Mental Health Check In, the user has to answer questions using a rating scale (1–5), and the system provides custom advice based on their scores. This was done by using if and else statements. Based on the number you selected, there will be custom feedback provided to help improve your mental health.

Lastly, for the Forums section, we used a variable to store the input you type in the text box. This variable is in a class that is then displayed in the forum, so you can see what you and other people typed. This forum page allows people of all ages and genders to connect in a safe environment.

Healthy steps to improve your mental health

Mental health is a really important thing that we all need to focus on in our daily lives. Even though this may be hard to do, due to school and work, it is key to living a healthy life. So here are some small healthy habits you can take to improve your mental health:

Firstly, make sure you get enough sleep, which is around 7-8 hours daily. This allows your brain to rest and process emotions better. This helps reduce stress and anxiety. Try to also sleep by 9-ish every night and not stay awake till 11 pm, as it can be very detrimental to your body if done constantly.

Second, maintaining an active lifestyle is crucial. Playing sports, exercising, walking, and stretching are examples of activities that can improve mental clarity and energy levels. Try to do this for at least 1 hour a day to keep your mental health good.

Thirdly, diet is crucial. To sustain consistent energy levels and enhance brain function, try to eat fruits, vegetables, and protein. Alongside this, try to consume 1.5 litres of water per day. If you occasionally eat fast food, that's okay, but try not to make it a habit, and create an equal balance with the amount of unhealthy and healthy food you consume.
