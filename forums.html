Forum Rules and Guidelines

To keep this a safe and supportive space, please follow these core principles whenever you post or reply.

  1. Respect Everyone: Treat all members with courtesy. Personal attacks, harassment, hate speech, and impersonation are not allowed.
  2. Remain Supportive: This is a space for support, not harm. Do not post content that could endanger yourself or others.
  3. Protect Privacy: Do not share personal identifying details about yourself or anyone else (full names, addresses, phone numbers, etc.).
